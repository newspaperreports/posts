Crime

Parents and aunt killed their own daughter to grab 32 bighas of land and trap neighbors.

Newspaper reports (Phulbari, Kurigram)
Criminal

The girl was asleep at night. Her parents were also in Dena's room. Then, around midnight, her parents called her daughter, Jannat, and said, "Jannat."

I woke up and said, "Let's go out for a bit."

The girl was surprised by her sleepiness. She kept asking where she was going so late at night. Her parents, without giving any answer, kept forcing her to go out.

Since her parents say so, the girl doesn't say no anymore. She goes out with them with sleep in her eyes and curiosity.

After going a little further, the girl noticed that her aunt Shahina Begum was also going with them. The girl thought that maybe they were all going together for some important work.

But after going a little further, the girl noticed her parents leaving their home road and heading towards farmland.

The girl was surprised and asked why they had come here. But her parents and aunt told her to be quiet. The girl's eyes were still sleepy.

After a while, they came to a cornfield. They were curious and wanted to ask their parents and aunt why they were here so late at night.

But before that, he grabbed Jannati's hands and mouth from behind so she couldn't scream. Then he hit her hard on the head and body with the rod. But after going a little further, the girl noticed her parents leaving their home road and heading towards farmland.

Then, her parents and aunt Shahina Begum started beating the girl with sharp sticks. The girl was very weak and was moaning in intense pain.

The three of them continued to beat the girl, blood spread everywhere. Then the girl died there.

As part of a pre-planned plan, the parents and aunt killed the girl and left her to eat the corn. The incident happened on Saturday.

The next morning, when local farmers went to work in the fields, they found Jannati's body there.

When he saw the body, he went to find his parents. They came and pretended to cry for a long time so that no one would understand.

Then the girl's uncle and parents filed a case against 27 neighbors. The police immediately arrested those neighbors and started questioning them.

But the police could not solve anything. Everyone was in denial. The police interrogated them more forcefully, but it was of no use.

Two days later, the police conducted an autopsy on the body and found some clues. Based on those clues, the police brought the girl's parents and uncle to the police station for questioning.

They arrived at the police station and began testifying against those neighbors and demanding that they be hanged.

My parents kept saying that my daughter was killed by those neighbors. But the police ignored them and continued their normal investigation.

At one point, when Zorlo began questioning and brought up clues, Aunt Shahina Begum confessed to the police that they were the ones who killed the girl on Saturday night.

My parents still didn't admit it. But after Aunt Shahina Begum's confession, my parents realized there was no other way to survive.

Then the girl's father, Zahidul Islam, and mother, Morsheda Begum, also admitted that the three of them had brutally killed the girl.

Then, when asked the reason for the killing, they said that they killed their own daughter to frame their rival neighbors.

They had been having a dispute with their neighbors over a 32-bigha piece of land for a long time. So, Aunt Shahina Begum gave them a plan to kill her daughter and leave her on that land.

Then they will file a case in the name of their neighbors.This way, if the neighbors are jailed in the case, they can easily take over the 32 bighas of land and make it their own.

That is why they filed a case against 27 neighbors so that the entire family would be jailed.These confessions were collected, verified and authenticated by the Officer-in-Charge of Kurigram Sadar Police Station, Md. Habibullah.

Moreover, the police have also taken a confession from the parents about how the girl was killed.The girl, Jannati, is not very old, only 15 years old. She was studying in class nine.

Everyone in the village was very surprised by this incident and were crying in grief for the girl, saying that the girl's behavior was very good.

Everyone in the village was very surprised by this incident. The girl's school teachers and classmates also said that the girl was very good in studies.
